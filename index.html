FAQ

  • How does Auren work?

    You share what you're going through — a struggle, a feeling, a moment of gratitude — and Auren generates a prayer that reflects it honestly. No scripts, no generic language. Just something real.

  • Do I need a subscription?

    Your first moment is free. After that, Auren requires a subscription to keep coming back. New subscribers get a 3-day free trial before any charge.

  • How do I cancel my subscription?

    You can cancel anytime through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Cancelling stops future charges and keeps your access active until the end of the billing period.

  • Is my data private?

    What you share in Auren is used only to generate your prayer in that moment. We don't store your personal entries or sell your data to anyone.
